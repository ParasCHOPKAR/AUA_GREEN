About Us

At Aquagreen Tech Solutions, we are committed to making a positive impact on the environment by providing innovative and sustainable solutions in water treatment, wastewater treatment, energy management, water audits, industrial fabrication, and water treatment chemicals.



With our slogan “Reduce • Recycle • Reuse”, we promote responsible and eco-friendly practices to safeguard natural resources for future generations.

Our Vision

Aquagreen Tech Solutions envisions a world where water is utilized wisely and efficiently, leading to a healthier and greener planet. We aim to be at the forefront of water and energy industries, driving change through cutting-edge technologies and ethical practices.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the water and energy sectors with comprehensive and customized solutions. We prioritize sustainability, efficiency, and cost-effectiveness to maximize client performance while reducing environmental impact.
