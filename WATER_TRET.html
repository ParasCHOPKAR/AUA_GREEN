Water Treatment Solutions

We provide innovative and sustainable water treatment solutions designed to meet the needs of industries, municipalities, and communities. Our solutions ensure clean, safe, and eco-friendly water for all applications.

Ozone System

High-efficiency ozone systems for disinfection and purification of water in various industries.

Ultrafiltration (UF) System

Reliable UF systems for removing suspended solids, bacteria, and viruses from water.

Water Treatment Plant (WTP)

Complete water treatment plants tailored for municipal and industrial water purification needs.

Demineralization (DM) Plant

Advanced DM plants for removal of dissolved minerals and salts to produce high-purity water.

Reverse Osmosis (RO) Plant

High-performance RO systems for industrial and residential water purification applications.

Water Softener

Efficient water softening systems to prevent scale formation and improve water quality.

Aquagreen Tech Solutions provides sustainable innovations in water & wastewater treatment, ensuring a greener and cleaner future for industries and communities.
