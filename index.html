About Us

Aquagreen Tech Solutions (AGTS) creates sustainable, innovative, and eco-friendly solutions in water treatment, wastewater treatment, energy management, water audits, industrial fabrication, and treatment chemicals.

Guided by Towards Sustainability, we protect natural resources for future generations. ...because we care for you!

Recognitions

We are proudly licensed by the Maharashtra Pollution Control Board, have obtained Consent to Establish, and maintain Consent to Operate, which is renewable periodically.

Industries We Serve
