Our Products & Solutions

Ozone System

The Ozone system reduces organic matter, COD/BOD load, disinfects odour & colour. It generates ozone using UV or corona discharge and oxidizes pollutants effectively.

Ultrafiltration (UF) System

Low-maintenance membrane process that removes bacteria, viruses & colloidal matter. Produces consistent water quality with 90‚Äì100% pathogen removal.

Water Treatment Plant

Advanced oxidation systems for safe drinking water. Combines coagulation, filtration & disinfection with UF/NF/RO for pure, healthy water.

DM Plant

Cutting-edge technology to remove impurities for industrial & human consumption. Built with durability & compliance in mind, backed by full support & AMC.

Reverse Osmosis (RO) Plant

  • Removes 99% contaminants
  • Energy-efficient operation
  • Smart monitoring & alerts
  • Durable & low maintenance

Water Softener

Ion exchange resin-based softening system with regeneration & brine tanks. Ensures scale-free, smooth water for industrial & domestic use.

Effluent Treatment Plant (ETP)

Employs MBBR, MBR, SBR & FAB technologies for industrial effluent purification, ensuring compliance with environmental standards.

Sewage Treatment Plant (STP)

Multi-stage process with ASP, MBBR, MBR, SBR & RBC. Energy-efficient, modular, and designed for safe water reuse.

Dissolved Air Flotation (DAF)

Removes oils & solids by tiny air bubbles. Widely used in Dairy, Paper, Oil, Refineries & Textile industries.

Clarifiers

Gravity settling system for suspended solids, producing sludge & scum removal with enhanced coagulants.

Wet Fume Scrubber

Neutralizes corrosive gases & particulate matter in compact installations. Safe handling of high-temp gas streams.

Multi Effect Evaporator (MEE)

Energy-efficient cascading evaporation system with automation, robust design & customizable configurations.

Comprehensive & Non-Comprehensive AMC

End-to-end maintenance of WTP, STP, ETP, MEE, RO & UF systems for continuous, efficient operations.

Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR)

Registrations & compliance services for Plastic Waste, E-Waste & Battery Waste management.

Central Ground Water Authority (CGWA) NOC

NOC facilitation for industries, borewells & authorized water suppliers for ground water extraction.

Water & Waste Water Treatment Chemicals

Lime/alum replacement, boiler & cooling tower chemicals, RO antiscalants, odor removers, bio-cultures & flocculants.

Environmental Monitoring

Ambient Air & Noise Monitoring, Stack Emission, Ventilation survey, DG Noise monitoring, STP/ETP sample analysis.
